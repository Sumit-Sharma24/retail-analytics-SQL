# 📊 Retail Sales & Customer Analytics

## 📌 Business Problem
Understand customer behavior, product performance, and sales trends to identify opportunities for improving revenue and retention.

---

## 📊 Overview
Analyzed 5,000+ transactions across 1,000 customers and 200 products using SQL. The project focuses on identifying patterns in purchasing behavior, sales performance, and growth trends.

---

## 🔍 Key Insights
- Identified **low repeat purchase rate** with 130 one-time customers  
- Top 10 products contribute only **~10% of total revenue**, indicating distributed demand  
- Monthly sales show **high volatility**, with fluctuating growth trends  
- Small group of high-value customers contributes significantly to revenue
